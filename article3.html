Best AI Tools for Everyday Use in 2025

AI is now part of our daily lives. Here are the top AI tools you can use today for free or low cost:

  • ChatGPT – Your AI assistant for writing, research, learning, and creativity.
  • Canva AI – Instantly generate designs, images, and marketing content.
  • Grammarly – AI-powered grammar and tone checker to improve your writing.
  • Google Bard – A Google-powered AI that helps with information search and answers.
  • Otter.ai – AI for transcribing meetings and voice notes in real-time.

These tools save time and boost your productivity — give them a try!
