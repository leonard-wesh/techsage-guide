How to Secure Your Smartphone in 2025

With cyber threats on the rise, protecting your smartphone has never been more important. Here are key steps to take:

  • Always use a strong passcode or biometric lock (fingerprint or face recognition).
  • Keep your phone updated with the latest security patches.
  • Only install apps from trusted sources like Google Play or Apple App Store.
  • Use two-factor authentication on important accounts.
  • Consider installing antivirus apps like Bitdefender or Norton Mobile Security.
